# Welcome to Ode

Ode is a creative music playlist manager that transforms your YouTube playlists into beautiful, shareable visual cards. Create custom music collections, design unique card appearances, and share your musical taste with the world.

## Key features

### Visual card creation

Create stunning visual cards for your playlists with customizable backgrounds, colors, and emojis. Each card represents a curated collection of songs that can be easily shared and played.

### YouTube integration

Simply paste YouTube links to add songs to your cards. Ode automatically extracts video IDs and fetches song titles, supporting various YouTube URL formats including:
- Standard YouTube URLs (`youtube.com/watch?v=`)
- Short URLs (`youtu.be/`)
- YouTube Music URLs (`music.youtube.com/watch?v=`)
- Mobile URLs (`m.youtube.com/watch?v=`)
- Embed URLs (`youtube.com/embed/`)

### Customizable appearance

Personalize your cards with:
- Custom colors or blurred song thumbnails as backgrounds
- Emoji or text-based card heroes
- Drag-and-drop song reordering
- Title customization

### Keyboard-first workflow

Navigate and control playback efficiently with keyboard shortcuts:
- **Space**: Play/pause
- **h/l**: Previous/next track
- **j/k**: Seek backward/forward 10 seconds
- **c**: Create new card
- **o**: Toggle pull-out player
- **Esc**: Close card crafter

### Download and share

Export your cards as `.card` files that contain all song information and visual preferences. Share your cards with others who can import them into their own Ode instance.

<Note>
  Ode is a **desktop-only application**. Mobile devices are not currently supported. Make sure you're accessing Ode from a desktop or laptop computer for the best experience.
</Note>

## What you'll create

With Ode, you'll create portable music card files that include:
- Your playlist title and visual settings
- A curated list of YouTube songs with custom titles
- Background styling (solid colors or blurred thumbnails)
- Hero elements (emojis or text)

These cards can be played directly in Ode's player interface or shared with others to experience your musical curation.
